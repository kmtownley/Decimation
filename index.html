How To Decimate:

Your mission, which you should definitely choose to accept, is to match the decimal word with the correct floating blossom. If you choose wisely, your blossom will explode and you will gain 10 points. However, choose incorrecty, and your score will drop by 10 points. To begin, press the play button. And may the blossoms be ever in your favor.

Want a new game? Simply press the game controller button. To end the game press the stop button but be careful! You will lose your progress.
